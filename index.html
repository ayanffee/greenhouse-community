Who We Are

A Greenhouse for
Well-Rounded Women

The Greenhouse Community is a safe, nurturing space where women come to grow — in their faith, their minds, their careers, and their relationships. We believe every woman carries seeds of greatness that, when nurtured in the right environment, will bloom into extraordinary fruit.

Founded on faith and fueled by sisterhood, we provide the soil, sunlight, and support every woman needs to become the best version of herself.

Our Promise

What We Commit to You

When you step into The Greenhouse, you step into a covenant community — one that pledges to walk with you every step of the way.

🌱

A Safe Space to Grow

No judgment, no comparison. Only genuine encouragement, honest conversations, and space to be exactly where you are in your journey.

🤝

Sisterhood & Accountability

You won't walk alone. We connect you with like-minded women who will hold you accountable, celebrate your wins, and support you through challenges.

📚

Growth Resources

Access to curated workshops, masterclasses, mentorship, and tools designed to help you grow in every dimension of your life.

✝️

Faith-Centred Foundation

Everything we do is grounded in God's Word. We believe true, lasting growth starts from the inside out — rooted in faith.

🌿

Practical Life Skills

From career development to emotional intelligence, we equip you with real, actionable skills for everyday life and long-term success.

👑

Celebrating Every Woman

We see you, we honour your story, and we celebrate who you are becoming. In this community, every woman is a queen.

Our Pillars

The Four Foundations

Our community is built on four interconnected pillars that together form the blueprint for a well-rounded, flourishing life.

01

Faith & Spirituality

Deepening your relationship with God through scripture study, prayer, and Spirit-led living. A strong spirit is the root of everything.

02

Mind & Wellness

Cultivating mental health, emotional intelligence, and a mindset that positions you for the life you desire.

03

Career & Purpose

Discovering your unique calling, building your skills, and creating a career that aligns with who God made you to be.

04

Relationships & Community

Building healthy, meaningful relationships — with God, yourself, and others — that bring out the very best in you.

The Founder

Meet Queen
Adelowokan

Queen Adelowokan is a passionate advocate for women's growth, wholeness, and purpose. Driven by a deep faith and a calling to see women flourish, she founded The Greenhouse Community to create the kind of safe, nurturing environment she herself had always longed for.

A speaker, mentor, and community builder, Queen brings warmth, wisdom, and a genuine love for women to everything she does. Her vision is simple yet profound: that every woman would know her worth, walk in her purpose, and grow into the fullness of who God created her to be.

Through The Greenhouse Community, Queen is cultivating a generation of well-rounded women who are rooted in faith, equipped for life, and boldly becoming.
